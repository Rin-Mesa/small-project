Welcome to PNC Cambodia

Passerelles Numériques Cambodia was the first project to be launched in 2005, in Phnom Penh. PNC offers two IT
training programs in System and Network Administration (SNA) and Web Programming (WEP), based on a holistic educative approach including
technical and soft skills, and a personal development program.

VALUE OF PNC

Solidarity

Supporting and helping each other as one family.
Building strong teamwork and unity among
students, staff, and community.

Trust

Being honest and reliable in words and actions.
Creating confidence between students, staff, and partners.

Respect

Treating everyone with dignity, kindness, and fairness.
Valuing diversity and encouraging positive communication.

Responsibility

Taking ownership of actions, studies, and commitments.
Being accountable for personal growth and contributing to society.
